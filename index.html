Kick Start Pre-Work

Before we start building your next level, we need to get clear on where you are now, what you actually want, and what keeps pulling you back.

This pre-work gives you a starting point. You will use these answers throughout the program to shape your goals, identity statements, habits, systems and action plan. Here are some guidelines for completion:

  • This is not a test. It is not about perfect answers. It is about honest answers.
  • Don't write what sounds impressive. Write what is true.
  • Tap the i button next to any question for information and example answers.
  • Done is better than perfect. Take approximately 30 minutes to complete this.
  • Tap Save and email me my answers at any point to get a link sent to you – you can return and edit any time.

Part 1 – The Life You Actually Want

1 If your business gave you back 10 hours a week, what would you actually do with that time?
2 What would a great ordinary week look like outside the business?
3 What do you want the people closest to you to experience from you this year?

Part 2 – The Business That Supports It

4 What is one business result that would make the biggest difference this year?
5 What work are you still doing that the business should no longer rely on you for?
6 Describe the type of client, job or work you want more of – and the type you want less of.

Part 3 – The Owner You Need to Become

7 What kind of owner or leader does the next version of your business need you to become?
8 What habit, if done consistently for 90 days, would change your business or life?

Part 4 – What Keeps Pulling You Back

9 What keeps slowing you down, distracting you, or pulling you back into old patterns?
10 What belief, fear, excuse or story might be keeping you stuck at your current level?
